# Sessions

The Sessions view is where you see how you actually work with your agents, one
[session](/glossary#session) at a time, rather than as totals. Open any session to
read what happened.

## Browsing sessions

The Sessions list shows every session Argus has indexed, newest first, with its
date, project, tokens and cost. Filter and sort to find the one you want.

## Inside a session

Opening a session shows its detail: the prompts you sent, how long it ran, the
tokens and cost it used, the tools it called and a summary of what it did.
